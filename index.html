Tips and Tricks on Organization

Jonathan Villalobos

Hello, My name is Jonathan (Prefer Jon) and these are my prefered tips and tricks of taking control of time. I am very fond of making sure that I have an organized day by writing down what I need to accomplish by the end of the week and finishing up my list early means more personal free time.

In the main body of this website, you will see a list of things that I tested or did my research on to have some help when making a list of sorts. Depending on what you are looking for, I am hoping that my personal experience if not research, on this Software To-Do list can help you personally to become a well-organized person overall.

Helpful Videos

To-Do Lost Software

Todoist

The app can be used for personal and professional purposes, and has features like task delegation, calendar integration, and goal tracking. It can be downloaded for free on most platforms, or you can upgrade to a paid plan if you want more features.

To-Do List Software

TickTcik

With it, you can manage tasks comprehensively, automate reminders, prioritize tasks, and integrate with calendars. It also comes with unique features, such as a Pomodoro timer and voice input.

To-Do Calendar

Google Calendar

An extremely popular Google app that integrates seamlessly with other Google apps. It features features such as reminders, shared calendars, and event invitations. This is very common in everone laptop/desktop if they have a windows and it is a great alternative if you don't prefer the next option which is another Email/Calendar software. It is very easy to learn how to use it and there are a lot of videos going in depth on how to use it.

To-Do Calendar

Microsoft Outlook Calendar

This professional-grade tool can be plugged into the Microsoft ecosystem and is excellent for collaboration. It can also be integrated with email for simple scheduling. I use this alot and it has made my time of making schedules easier than before while also keeping a close eye on my emails.

Shopping List Software

AnyList

It's easy to create and organize shopping lists with this user-friendly app. You can also collaborate in real time with family members or roommates.

Shopping List Software

Out Of Milk

You can use this app to manage grocery lists, pantry inventories, and to-do lists, so you never forget important things again
