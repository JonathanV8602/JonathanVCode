Menu

  • Nibbles
    Appetizer
    Your choice of 2 pieces of either Croissant, Chocolate Croissant, or Mini Cream Puff. These little nibbles are a perfect way to start your meal with an appetizer. This can give you a warm welcome to our Cafe with a freshly baked Croissant or a fresh Mini Cream Puff covered in powdered sugar, starting at $5.49 for 2.
  • Fancy Feast
    The Main Course
    We offer a choice of 3 sandwiches. The first is a simple turkey and cheese sandwich with your choice of toppings. The second is an egg salad sandwich. The third, a hot option, is a sausage or bacon, egg, and cheese sandwich on your choice of bread, starting at $10.99.
  • Treats
    The Finalizer
    We offer 3 choices of treats: Home-made Tres Leches or Tiramisu slices, pre-sliced and ready to eat at $5 per slice. Our third option is a slice of apple or cherry pie, also priced at $5 per slice.
  • Drink
    Quench your thirst
    We offer hot or cold coffee, ranging in size from small to extra large, starting at $3.49 for a small. We also have fresh bottled orange juice, apple juice, or water. Apple or orange juice is priced at $3.99, and water at $1.50. We also serve Matcha tea, hot or cold!

    • Please inform our staff of any food allergies or sensitivities before placing your order.
